About Us

Nava Raipur Atal Nagar Vikas Pradhikaran (NRANVP), envisioned as the capital of Chhattisgarh after it attained statehood on 1st November 2000, is located 24 km from Raipur. As a satellite city, it offers a blank slate for planned urban development, including sustainable and inclusive urban transport systems.

Urban transport demand emerged with the relocation of the State Secretariat and Government offices in 2013. Residential development in four key sectors followed, with the population gradually increasing. Current transport demand is characterized by commuting patterns between Raipur, Nava Raipur Atal Nagar, and nearby towns, especially during peak AM and PM hours. A more uniform intra-city transport demand is expected as the city matures with operational social, commercial, and healthcare facilities, and more residents settle in.

BRT Lite Nava Raipur Atal Nagar named “Tatpar” was launched by Hon’ble Prime Minister on 1st November 2016. The BRT infrastructure are unique in design and quality of construction is high. The BRT buses are BS-IV compliant, fully AC, and Ultra-Modern in look and performance with automatic transmission. They are also IT-equipped with features like CCTV surveillance for enhanced safety and route information displays for a seamless travel experience. The Buses operate at every 15 minute frequency at peak hours and at every 30 minute frequency at peak hours from 6.00 AM to 10.00 PM. The fleet size comprises of 30 nos. USB-II compliant AC BRT Buses which ply in mixed traffic in Raipur and in soft marked corridor in Nava Raipur Atal Nagar. The Buses have RFID assisted docking facilities in the median shelters and also have facilities for kerb side boarding. The Buses and Shelters have CCTV Surveillance System. The buses and the shelters allow barrier free movement of wheel chairs. Fare Matrix is competitive with that of Non AC Buses so as to keep it affordable and accessible to all section of riders. The operation is successful with Zero Accident System. The design and construction of cycle track and pedestrian pathway and crossings are made such that especially abled persons can easily use them. The project has a vital role to implement Nava Raipur Atal Nagar vision of ‘Mobility of all, affordable, equitable and safe’.

The BRTS Lite System ensures seamless multi-modal connectivity across the city. The BRT routes are strategically planned to connect major transit hubs, including Raipur Railway Station at Raipur, CBD Railway Station at Nava Raipur Atal Nagar, and inter-state bus stops along NH-30 and NH-53. Additionally, the system enhances last-mile connectivity to residential and commercial sectors through e-Autos and a Public Bicycle Sharing (PBS) network, promoting efficient and sustainable urban mobility.

Features that Make Travel Convenient

  • 01 Buses: Over 30 AC buses with doors on both sides and more standing space, that ply smoothly and rapidly in reserved lanes.
  • 02 Bus stations in the BRT Lanes: Covered and provide protection from rain and sun, well lit, have a ramp at the entrance and signage boards with information about the BRT corridors.
  • 03 Tickets at stations: The ticket is to be bought at the ticket counter through ETMs (Cash or UPI) inside the station before boarding the bus.
  • 04 Level Boarding: The height of the bus platform and the BRT station platform are at the same level. Passengers do not have to climb steps to board the bus, a feature similar to metro rail.
  • 05 Automatic Doors: Automatic doors on BRT stations and bus doors open only when the bus is properly docked at the station.
  • 06 Crossings: The crossings from the footpath to the BRT stations have signals in many locations or have speed tables so that vehicles have to slow down to allow passengers to cross safely.
  • 07 Bus Numbers, Bus Arrival and Station Information:
    i. Display screens and audio announcements in buses give information about the next stop
    ii. Route numbers appear on LED displays on the front, back and the left side of buses
  • 08 Intelligent Transit Management System: BRT Buses have GPS and all buses and stations are linked with the BRTS Control Room which tracks bus movement and gives feedback to drivers to improve service.
  • 09 Smooth Ride & Convenient Journey: Our buses are designed for a smooth and comfortable journey, allowing you to relax and enjoy the ride.
  • 10 Panoramic Views: Large glass windows offer passengers a panoramic view, allowing them to enjoy the lush greenery of Nava Raipur during their commute.
  • 11 Enhanced Safety: An SOS button is installed inside the buses for immediate assistance in case of an emergency.

Benefits of Tatpar BRT

The Tatpar BRTS is being developed as the sustainable public transport option for Raipur to Nava Raipur Atal Nagar and within Nava Raipur Atal Nagar. The rapid increase in vehicular traffic, increased congestion, expanded city limits and longer travel distances are some important trends in the twin cities. A high quality public transport is essential to avoid adverse impacts on the health of people and quality of life.

BRTS system is developed for safe, affordable with the focus on commuter comfort and convenience, integration of modes and information technology. These would help move towards a livable and sustainable future.

Tatpar BRTS aims to provide a few immediate benefits to passengers – Comfortable ride on the BRTS buses, with shorter waiting times and shorter travel time. The long term benefits to Nava Raipur Atal Nagar urban areas expected are:

  • Reduced pollution from motor vehicles
  • Reduced congestion due to motor vehicles
  • Reduced spending on infrastructure for motor vehicles such as flyovers, parking lots
  • Increased economic activity due to improved connectivity
